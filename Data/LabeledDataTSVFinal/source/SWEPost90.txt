The PositionDo you dream about creating a more sustainable future? At Uplight, we are motivating energy users and providers to accelerate the clean energy ecosystem. Working with over 90 of the worlds leading electric and gas utilities, Uplight provides an end-to-end customer energy experience. Uplight delivers personalized experiences that customers have now come to expectimproving... satisfaction, increasing revenue, reducing the cost to serve, and contributing to carbon reduction goals. We are B Corp certified, enabling us to put our values into action by not only making decisions for the benefit of our shareholders, but also for our customers, environment, employees, and community.We are seeking a Senior Software Engineer to join our team and help us achieve our ambitious goals for our business and the planet.What you get to do: Co-own development of our internal Device Integration service using Python microservices to interface with other internal services and external APIs. Your team will be responsible for developing clear, uniform interfaces to enable our peer teams to integrate with 3rd-party OEM APIs for residential devices including thermostats, electric vehicle chargers, and electric water heaters from Nest, Ecobee, and other major OEMs. Together, we will design and build reliable, scalable and extensible services that allow our applications to communicate with hundreds of thousands of connected devices in residential homes.Skills and experience are necessary, but we hire on value alignment first, so if you feel you would be a good fit with us, still consider applying.What you will contribute: Youll be a part of our Engineering Department, developing robust architecture and leading large feature initiatives that deliver a compelling experience for our customers in the area of Demand Response and Energy Orchestration, in service of enabling a more flexible energy grid and shifting energy consumption to save carbon emissions and energy costs for real humans at scale. As part of an organization-wide initiative to align teams and streamline communication, our team is forming to assume responsibility for existing device integration services as the prior team transitions to another product space. Our growing team currently includes a Staff Engineer with deep experience in Python integrating with APIs, and decomposing large applications into smaller services, with several additional mid-to-senior level engineers from within Uplight to join the team throughout 2023.What you bring to Uplight: 4+ years of professional software engineering experience Advanced knowledge of Python Experience building and managing APIs to industry-accepted RESTful standards 3+ years building maintainable integration and unit tests Affinity for synchronous and asynchronous collaboration via pair programming, peer review, brainstorming, etc. Experience facilitating collaboration cross-functionally with Design and Product teamsBonus points: Experience implementing scalable microservice architecture Experience mentoring peers, both technically and non-technically Excellence requires diversity: we strongly encourage candidates from underrepresented backgrounds in technology to apply.What makes working at Uplight amazing:In addition to all the standard medical and dental benefits, that kick in Day 1, we are: Proud to be over 500+ purpose-driven individuals helping to create a more sustainable planet.
