The Attachment Architects are responsible for all attachment related use-cases within the Smartsheet ecosystem. We collaborate with other teams to enable users of Smartsheet to attach files from several locations, providing services to download and replicate the attachments to a users destinations of choice. You will be working with 7-8 other engineers on the re-architecture, implementation... deployment and maintenance of the services and infrastructure owned by the team.
